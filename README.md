# caregiver-intervention-analysis
Favorability of behavior change technique text-based interventions and co-walking habits among caregivers of persons with Alzheimer Disease and AD-related Dementias (AD/ADRD)

Caregiver Intervention Analysis — Feinstein Institutes / Northwell Health

Healthcare analytics case study: statistical analysis of an NIH-funded
(P30AG063786) text-based walking intervention for caregivers of persons with
Alzheimer's disease and related dementias (N = 146).

My role (Research Intern, Institute of Health System Science, Summer 2026):
survey data cleaning, scale reliability checks, non-parametric statistical
testing, bootstrapped correlation analysis, and co-authoring the poster below.
Findings will inform the design of future NIH-funded caregiver interventions.

➡️ analysis_demo.md — the full statistical pipeline in R,
demonstrated on simulated data (participant data is confidential): Cronbach's
alpha, Friedman test with Kendall's W, Bonferroni-corrected pairwise Wilcoxon
tests with rank-biserial effect sizes, Mann-Whitney U, and Spearman
correlations with BCa bootstrapped confidence intervals.
